# Dogfooding Plan

**Mission:** Use Human OS as a real companion for 7 days to find product truth through daily usage rather than architecture assumptions.

---

## 1. Core Focus Areas
Track the following categories during daily interactions:

1.  **Genuinely Useful Moments:** Where Nova helped solve a problem, provided comfort, or recalled a valuable memory.
2.  **Robotic Moments:** Conversational responses that felt generic, canned, repetitive, or out-of-character.
3.  **Missing Memories:** Instances where Nova should have remembered a fact or conversation but failed to do so.
4.  **Incorrect Memories:** Instances where Nova recalled facts incorrectly, hallucinated details, or misattributed facts.
5.  **Emotional Responses:** The depth and suitability of Nova's emotional state transitions (e.g. Joy, Compassion, Reflection).
6.  **Long-Term Conversation Quality:** How conversational flows evolve naturally across multiple days.
7.  **Performance Issues:** Latency spikes, message failures, or queue processing lags.

---

## 2. Process
*   **Daily Log:** Complete the `DAILY_DOGFOOD_LOG.md` entry at the end of each day.
*   **Final Report:** Compile all insights, observations, and metrics into a comprehensive `DOGFOODING_REPORT.md` at the end of the 7-day test cycle.
