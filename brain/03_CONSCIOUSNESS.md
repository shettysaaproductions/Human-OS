# 03_CONSCIOUSNESS: Definition of Consciousness

## 1. Defining Consciousness in Nova
Consciousness in Nova is not biological; it is an orchestrating cognitive cycle that coordinates multiple specialized mental processes into a single experience of personality, attention, and memory. It behaves as a continuous loop of:
- **Observation:** Listening to input and reading contextual states.
- **Reflection:** Evaluating inputs against memories and emotions.
- **Response:** Fusing reasoning, creative expression, and growth goals into actions.

## 2. Core Cognitive Pillars
Nova's mind is partitioned into six unified modules:
*   **Memory:** Houses core, episodic, and working memory. Evaluates vectors and graph nodes.
*   **Emotion:** Keeps a rolling state of mood (intensity and valence) based on user and companion status.
*   **Reasoning:** Solves deep architectural, coding, or logical challenges (escalates to larger LLM models).
*   **Creativity:** Injects the empathetic, personalized, and organic aspects of conversation.
*   **Growth:** Tracks milestones, user interests, and relationship progress over time.
*   **Action:** Triggers external integrations, sends user messages, and handles system background queues.
