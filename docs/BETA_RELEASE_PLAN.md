# BETA RELEASE PLAN

## Phase 1: Internal Testing
**Audience:** Core team (1-5 users).
**Goal:** Verify critical paths (Auth, Chat, Memory, Push OTA).
**Duration:** 2-3 days.

## Phase 2: Friends & Family
**Audience:** Known contacts (5-10 users).
**Goal:** Identify obvious UX friction, test edge-case inputs.
**Duration:** 1 week.

## Phase 3: Closed Beta
**Audience:** Waitlist / Invites (10-50 users).
**Goal:** Stress test backend, monitor crash rates, collect active feedback.
**Duration:** 2 weeks.

## Success Metrics
- **Crash Free Sessions:** > 99%
- **Daily Active Users:** Increasing engagement over time
- **User Feedback:** Positive sentiment, actionable feature requests
- **Retention:** Day 1 and Day 7 retention baselines established
