# ACCOUNT DELETION POLICY

**Last Updated:** [Insert Date]

## 1. How to Request Deletion
Users can request the deletion of their HumanOS account and associated data by:
- Navigating to **Settings > Account > Delete Account** within the app.
- Emailing us directly at [Insert Contact Email] with the subject line "Account Deletion Request".

## 2. What Data is Deleted
Upon receiving a verified deletion request, we will permanently delete:
- Your authentication credentials.
- Your entire chat history and AI conversation logs.
- Your saved preferences and settings.

## 3. Retention Timelines
- Account deletion requests initiated in-app are processed immediately.
- Email requests will be processed within 14 days of verification.
- Routine system backups may retain encrypted fragments of data for up to 30 days before being fully overwritten.

## 4. Exceptions
We may retain specific pieces of data if legally required to do so, or to resolve active disputes. Anonymized crash logs and aggregated usage statistics (not tied to your identity) are retained to improve app performance.
