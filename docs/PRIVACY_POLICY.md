## 1. Information We Collect
We collect the following information when you use HumanOS:
- **Account Information:** Email address and authentication details.
- **Chat Data:** Chat history, messages, and interactions with the AI.
- **User Preferences:** App settings and language preferences.
- **Device & Usage Data:** Crash reports, performance metrics, and temporary memory used to improve app stability.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
- To provide, maintain, and improve the HumanOS app.
- To process chat queries via backend APIs and external AI models.
- To monitor app stability and push Over-The-Air (OTA) updates.

## 3. Data Storage and Retention
Your data is securely stored on our backend infrastructure (powered by Supabase). We retain chat history and account data as long as your account is active. Temporary memory may be periodically cleared.

## 4. Third-Party Services
We use trusted third-party services to operate the app:
- **Supabase:** Backend and database hosting.
- **Expo / EAS:** OTA updates and app builds.
- **Sentry [Planned]:** Crash reporting and diagnostics.
- **AI Providers:** NVIDIA Llama, Gemini, OpenRouter (queries are processed by these services).
