# KNOWN LIMITATIONS

Below are the known limitations of the current beta version of HumanOS:

- **Native module upgrades**: Require a new APK to be built and installed; they cannot be pushed via OTA updates.
- **OTA updates**: Apply on the next app restart after downloading in the background.
- **Sentry**: Crash reporting requires the future native binary for full integration.
- **Backend downtime**: May affect AI companion responsiveness and performance.
- **Language preference**: Translation and local preference options depend on backend availability.
